Library scanner

Your whole collection, instantly cataloged.

Point BookCatalog at any folder and it recursively scans every ebook, pulling titles, authors, cover art, publishers, and more—automatically.

  • Recursive folder scanning
  • Auto-extracted cover art
  • Metadata from EPUB, PDF, MOBI, DOCX
  • New books appear as you add files

Beautiful browsing

Browse by cover, or drill into detail.

Switch between a visual cover grid and a compact list view with a single click. Search by title, author, or series—and filter by format, folder, or reading status.

  • Grid & list views
  • Fast full-text search
  • Filter by format, folder, status
  • Sort by title, author, date, rating

Everything included

Built for readers, not archivists.

All the essentials—without the learning curve of tools built for professionals.

Ratings & status
Rate books 1–5 stars and mark them as Unread, Reading, or Read. Your progress, at a glance.
Built-in EPUB reader
Read EPUBs right inside the app. No need to configure an external reader or leave BookCatalog.
Private notes
Jot down thoughts, quotes, or reminders on any book. Notes stay private on your machine.
Instant search
Find any book in your library in milliseconds. Search title, author, series, or publisher.
No cloud, ever
Everything runs locally. Your library data never leaves your computer. No internet required.
Up in 30 seconds
Download, install, pick a folder. That's it. No wizards, no accounts, no configuration.
