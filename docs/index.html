Everything you need

Built for book lovers, not power users

All the essentials for managing your personal library—without the learning curve of tools built for professional archivists.

Scan any folder
Point BookCatalog at your ebook directory. It recursively scans and imports every supported file—instantly.
Grid & List views
Browse your library as a beautiful cover grid or switch to a compact list for quick scanning. Toggle anytime.
Smart search & filter
Find any book in seconds. Search by title, author, or series. Filter by format, folder, or reading status.
Auto-extract metadata
Titles, authors, cover art, year, publisher, language—all pulled automatically from your ebook files.
Ratings & status
Rate books 1–5 stars and mark them as Unread, Reading, or Read. Add private notes to any book.
Built-in EPUB reader
Read EPUBs right inside the app. No need to open a separate application or configure an external reader.
